About Us

Your Helpful Neighborhood Hardware Store

At Katz Ace Hardware, we're more than just a hardware store - we're your neighbors. Our knowledgeable team is dedicated to providing personalized service and expert advice for all your home improvement projects.

As part of the Ace Hardware cooperative, we combine the buying power and resources of the world's largest hardware retailer with the personalized service of a locally-owned business. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or a professional contractor, we have the tools, supplies, and expertise to help you get the job done right.
